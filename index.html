⚡ Circuit Toolkit — EAL Support

Y4 IPC Science · Korean & Japanese bilingual cards

한국어 日本語
How to use: Print on card stock and cut along the dashed lines (laminate for reuse across classes). Give each EAL student the vocabulary set to keep on their desk during the demonstration, the sequence cards to order while watching, and the sentence frame cards to support their written procedure. The two blank cards at the end of the vocabulary set let students add a word in their own language for anything not covered here.
